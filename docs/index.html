How it works

three steps, on repeat
1

Watch

Open a drill. Look at the small reference and read the four-or-so steps. Total: ~30 seconds.

2

Practice

Pen and paper at your desk. Set a soft timer. Fill the page. Most drills are 10 minutes.

3

Mark done

Tick the success boxes. Tomorrow, the next drill. The site keeps your place; come back any time.

The journey

seven tiers · roughly 50–60 hours, total
P.S.

You can’t doodle wrong. The drills are practice, not performance. Bad pages are part of the deal. Date them, keep them, move on.
